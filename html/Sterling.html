Overview

Project Overview

The main goal of this project is to create a captivating and professional Squarespace website for a tech product company. The website will be carefully designed to engage visitors and provide them with a great user experience. It will incorporate the latest design trends and innovative features to showcase the company's offerings and make a lasting impression on potential clients. This website will be a powerful tool for the company to establish an online presence, attract a wider audience, and grow the business.


Duration

  • Overall: 4 months.

  • Discovery & Research: 2 months.

  • Design & testing: 2 months

Project Goals


  • Promote a groundbreaking technology to educate potential buyers and investors.

  • Raise the profile of the Inventor who created the patent.

  • Promote the Inventors book.

  • Collect email address & newsletter subscribers.

Tools

My Process

Competitve Analysis Overview


Feature Comparison

I observed and analyzed how this competitor presents their product on their website, in order to gain insights on the key areas to focus on while developing this website project.

Interviews


5 Users

I conducted interviews with five individuals with the primary aim of gaining insights into the specific features and content users desire on a technology product website. To facilitate this exploration, I developed an interview script containing eight open-ended questions, designed to uncover their needs, priorities, and address any pain points they may have.


Insights

After conducting the interview, I analyzed the user responses to identify their pain points, determine the desired features for the website, and gain insights into their typical website navigation patterns and average time spent on a website.
